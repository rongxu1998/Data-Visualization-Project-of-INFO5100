INFO 3300 Project 2: NBA Shooting

From the 2014-15 NBA season to the 2017-18 NBA season, the Cleveland Cavaliers led by Lebron James and the Golden State Warriors led by Stephen Curry went head-to-head in the NBA Finals four years in a row. Some basketball analysts say that Curry and the Warriors changed how basketball was played by utilizing the 3-point shot unlike it had ever been before. Our project visualizes the changes in shot selection and accuracy across NBA players in 30 teams over the span of these four historic years, demonstrating how the game of basketball has changed because of the 3-point shot.
